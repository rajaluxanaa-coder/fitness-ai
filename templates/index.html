About FitAI

Our Mission

FitAI was created to make personalized fitness accessible to everyone. Using cutting-edge AI technology, we generate custom workout plans tailored to your unique needs, goals, and available equipment. No more generic routines - every plan is as unique as you are.

The Technology

Powered by Groq's Llama 3.3 AI model, FitAI analyzes your fitness level, available equipment, and personal goals to create optimized 5-day workout schedules. Our intelligent system learns from your progress and adapts recommendations over time.
